BubbleChain Index (BCI)

ESG ETF

An exchange-traded index fund that invests exclusively in assets that meet sustainability and social responsibility criteria

BCI is designed to be an eco-friendly crypto ETF that stands out for its dynamic selection of eco-friendly cryptocurrencies. This approach allows for the integration of new green assets as they emerge, ensuring that BCI remains at the forefront of green innovation in the crypto sector.

This proactive and dynamic strategy positions BCI as a true forerunner in the ESG ETF space, offering investors a unique opportunity to participate in a scalable portfolio, aligned with technological advances and environmental imperatives.

🌱 What Makes BubbleChain Index Unique?

In a sector where the carbon footprint is a growing concern, BCI offers a green and profitable alternative, aligned with the new expectations of ESG investors and institutions.

🎯 Objective of the OFM:

✅ To offer investors a diversified basket of the most sustainable and energy-efficient cryptocurrencies.

✅ To meet the growing demand for financial products aligned with ESG criteria.

✅ Promote a greener, more efficient and responsible blockchain ecosystem.

✅ To generate long-term value for Index Coop and its investors through an optimised management and rebalancing strategy.
