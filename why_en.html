📊 Why Choose BubbleChain Index?

Cryptocurrencies have revolutionized finance, but their environmental impact remains a major concern. BubbleChain Index provides a solution by selecting only digital assets that meet ESG criteria.

🌍 The Current Issues with Cryptos

  • ⚡ High Energy Consumption: Bitcoin consumes more energy than entire countries like Argentina ([Cambridge CCAF](https://ccaf.io/cbeci/)).
  • 💨 Significant Carbon Footprint: Bitcoin mining generates 65 megatons of CO₂ per year, equivalent to Greece ([Nature Communications](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41558-021-01053-2)).
  • 📉 Lack of ESG Regulations: Most existing crypto ETFs do not apply strict ESG filters.

🌟 BubbleChain Index: A Sustainable Solution

  • ✅ Responsible Selection: Only blockchains using energy-efficient mechanisms like Proof-of-Stake are included.
  • ✅ Transparent Methodology: Analysis based on verifiable data on energy consumption and environmental impact.
  • ✅ ESG Compliance: Fully aligned with Socially Responsible Investment (SRI) standards.

📈 The Impact of ESG ETFs

ESG ETFs are growing rapidly, with $480 billion in assets under management in 2023, reflecting a +22% increase in one year ([ETFGI.com](https://www.etfgi.com)).
