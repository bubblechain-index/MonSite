🎯 Asset Selection Process

BCI is designed to include only the most sustainable and eco-friendly cryptos, while ensuring excellent financial performance.

⚖️ Crypto Selection Methodology

  • ✅ Energy efficiency: Priority to blockchains using Proof-of-Stake (PoS) or other green mechanisms.
  • ✅ Positive ESG impact: Commitment to carbon footprint reduction projects.
  • ✅ Adoption and innovation: Fast-growing blockchains integrated into real-world applications (finance, industry, etc.).

🚀 Example Cryptos Selected in BCI

  • 📌 Ethereum (ETH 2.0) – 99.9% more eco-friendly after the Proof-of-Stake transition.
  • 📌 Polygon (MATIC) – Ultra-efficient and energy-saving scaling solution.
  • 📌 Algorand (ALGO) – Blockchain certified "Carbon Negative".
  • 📌 Chia (XCH) – First protocol based on "Proof of Space and Time".
  • 📌 Hedera (HBAR) – 100,000x more energy-efficient than Bitcoin.
