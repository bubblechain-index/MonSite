📊 Asset Selection in BubbleChain Index

🌍 Why an ESG-Based Crypto Selection?

  • ⚡ High Energy Consumption: Bitcoin consumes **144 TWh/year**, more than Norway ([Cambridge CCAF](https://ccaf.io/cbeci/)).
  • 💨 Carbon Footprint: In 2022, Bitcoin mining generated **48.35 Mt of CO₂**, equivalent to Singapore ([Nature Communications](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41558-021-01053-2)).
  • 📈 Rising Demand: More investors seek assets that comply with ESG standards.

📋 Crypto Selection Criteria

  • ✅ Low Energy Consumption: Priority given to Proof-of-Stake blockchains.
  • ✅ Low Carbon Footprint: Assessment of CO₂ emissions per transaction.
  • ✅ Transparency: Only projects with public ESG reports are included.
  • ✅ ESG Commitment: Partnerships with sustainable initiatives.

🔍 Examples of Potentially Selected Cryptos

  • 💠 Ethereum (ETH) : Transitioned to Proof-of-Stake (-99.9% energy use).
  • 💠 Tezos (XTZ) : Known for its low carbon footprint.
  • 💠 Algorand (ALGO) : Carbon-neutral blockchain.

📌 These cryptos are examples and not a definitive list.
