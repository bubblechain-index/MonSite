💡 How Does an ESG Crypto ETF Work?

An **ETF (Exchange-Traded Fund)** is a publicly traded fund that tracks the performance of a set of assets.

For BubbleChain Index:

  • 📌 Selection of cryptos based on strict ESG criteria.
  • 📌 Simplified access to the crypto market without directly holding assets.
  • 📌 Diversification and optimized risk management.

💰 Revenue Model

Crypto ETFs generate revenue primarily through:

  • ✅ Management Fees: A small percentage (≈ 0.5 - 1%) charged on assets under management.
  • ✅ Market Performance: The ETF increases in value as selected cryptos appreciate.
  • ✅ Staking & Lending: Generating passive income through Proof-of-Stake assets.
